## Full Description
Banglu is a Bengali phonetic keyboard built for fast mobile typing.

Type Bengali using simple lowercase English letters. Banglu converts your phonetic input into clean Bengali text with smart suggestions, dictionary ranking, and confusing-letter handling for Bengali sounds like ত/ট, দ/ড, থ/ঠ, কি/কী, and c/ch mapping.

Main features:
• Lowercase-only Bengali phonetic typing
• Smart Bengali word suggestions
• Bengali voice typing with pause punctuation
• Full phonetic mapping tutorial
• Bengali and English keyboard modes
• Emoji panel and expression support
• Font size control
• Dark-first premium app design
• Local user preferences
• Works in WhatsApp, Messenger, Facebook, Chrome, Gmail, Notes, and other apps

Banglu is designed for Bengali users who want to type naturally without learning a complex keyboard layout. Just type how you think, and Banglu helps produce Bengali text quickly.

Privacy:
Banglu does not include ads or analytics. Core typing conversion works on your device. Internet is used for optional account login, dictionary sync, subscription/backend features, and network-dependent voice recognition. Voice typing uses your device's Android speech recognition service when you tap the microphone, and audio may be sent to that provider for transcription. Banglu does not store voice audio.

## Data Safety
Data collection:
Banglu does not collect keystrokes for ads or analytics. If the user signs in, Banglu may process account data, subscription state, sync metadata, and custom dictionary/conversion data needed for account and sync features.

Data sharing:
Banglu does not sell user data. Voice typing may send audio to the Android speech recognition provider selected by the user's device, such as Google, for transcription.

Security practices:
Core typing conversion is on-device. Account and sync requests use Banglu backend services only when the user signs in or uses sync.

Permissions:
• Vibration is used for keypress feedback.
• Microphone is used only when the user taps voice typing.

Voice typing note:
Banglu uses the Android speech recognition service available on the user's device. Audio may be sent to that speech provider for transcription. Banglu does not store audio recordings.
