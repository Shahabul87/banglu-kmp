# Privacy Policy — Banglu Bengali Keyboard

**Last updated:** March 23, 2026

## Overview

Banglu Bengali Keyboard ("the App") is a phonetic keyboard application that converts English letters to Bengali text on your device. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection

**We do NOT collect any data.** Specifically:

- **No keystroke logging:** We do not record, store, or transmit anything you type
- **No personal data collection:** We do not collect names, emails, or any personal information
- **No analytics:** We do not track usage patterns, app launches, or typing statistics
- **No network access:** The app does not require or use internet permission. All processing happens entirely on your device
- **No third-party services:** We do not integrate any advertising, analytics, or data collection SDKs

## Data Storage

The following data is stored **locally on your device only**:

- **Keyboard preferences:** Your settings (theme, haptic feedback, sound, etc.) are saved in the app's local storage
- **Learned words:** When you select a word suggestion, the app remembers your preference to improve future suggestions. This data never leaves your device
- **Dictionary data:** The Bengali dictionary (485,000 words) is bundled with the app and stored locally

## Permissions

The app requests only one permission:

- **VIBRATE:** Used for haptic feedback when pressing keys. This permission does not access any personal data.

## Data Sharing

We do **not** share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

## Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the changes here with an updated date.
