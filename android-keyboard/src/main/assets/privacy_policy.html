Banglu Keyboard - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Overview

Banglu Keyboard ("we", "our", "the app") is a Bengali phonetic keyboard for Android. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app collects and how it is used.

Data Collection

Banglu Keyboard does NOT collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

  • No keystrokes are recorded or transmitted. All phonetic conversion happens entirely on your device.
  • No internet connection is required or used. The app has no internet permission and makes zero network requests.
  • No analytics or tracking. We do not use any analytics SDKs, crash reporters, or advertising frameworks.
  • No personal information is collected. We do not collect names, emails, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information.

Data Stored on Device

The following data is stored locally on your device only and never leaves it:

  • Learned words: When you select a word from suggestions, the app remembers your preference to improve future suggestions. This is stored in Android SharedPreferences.
  • Settings: Your keyboard preferences (theme, haptic feedback, sound, etc.) are stored in SharedPreferences.
  • Dictionary: A pre-compiled Bengali dictionary (read-only) is bundled with the app for offline phonetic conversion.

Permissions

Banglu Keyboard requests only one permission:

  • VIBRATE: Used for haptic feedback when pressing keys. This can be disabled in the app settings.

Third-Party Services

Banglu Keyboard does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs.

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
