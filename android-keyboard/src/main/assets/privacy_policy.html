Banglu Keyboard - Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

Overview

Banglu Keyboard ("we", "our", "the app") is a Bengali phonetic keyboard for Android. Core keyboard typing and phonetic conversion work offline on your device and do not send typed text to Banglu's servers. Optional account, sync, subscription, and voice features may use network services as described below.

Data Collection

Banglu Keyboard does not sell personal data and does not collect keystrokes for advertising.

  • No keystroke logging for ads or analytics. Banglu does not record or transmit your typed keyboard text for advertising or analytics.
  • Offline core typing. Bengali phonetic conversion, local suggestions, and the bundled dictionary can work offline without a Banglu account.
  • Account data. If you create an account, Banglu may store your name, email, device identifier, authentication tokens, subscription state, and sync metadata needed for account features.
  • Dictionary sync. If you sign in and use sync, custom conversions and selected dictionary preferences may be sent to Banglu's backend so they can work across devices.
  • Voice typing is optional. When you tap the microphone, Banglu uses your device's Android speech recognition provider. Audio may be sent to that provider, such as Google, for transcription. Banglu does not store voice audio.
  • Internet may be used for account, sync, subscription, backend, and voice features. Core keyboard typing works offline.
  • No analytics or tracking. We do not use any analytics SDKs, crash reporters, or advertising frameworks.

Data Stored on Device

The following data is stored on your device. If you sign in and enable sync, selected account and dictionary data may be synced with Banglu's backend so your preferences can work across devices:

  • Learned words: When you select a word from suggestions, the app remembers your preference to improve future suggestions. This is stored in Android SharedPreferences.
  • Custom conversions: If you add your own typing formula, it is stored locally and may sync if you are signed in and use sync.
  • Clipboard history: If you open Banglu's clipboard panel, the app may save recent copied text locally so you can paste it again. You can clear clipboard history from the keyboard clipboard panel. Clipboard history is not sent to Banglu's backend.
  • Settings: Your keyboard preferences (theme, haptic feedback, sound, etc.) are stored in SharedPreferences.
  • Dictionary: A pre-compiled Bengali dictionary (read-only) is bundled with the app for offline phonetic conversion.

Permissions

Banglu Keyboard may request these permissions:

  • VIBRATE: Used for haptic feedback when pressing keys. This can be disabled in the app settings.
  • RECORD_AUDIO: Used only when you tap the microphone for voice typing.
  • INTERNET: Used for account login, sync, subscription/backend features, and network-dependent voice recognition.

Third-Party Services

Banglu does not integrate advertising or analytics SDKs. Voice typing uses the Android speech recognition service available on your device, which may be provided by Google or another system speech provider. Google sign-in and Google Play Billing may be used if you choose account or subscription features.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
